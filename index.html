About

Summary

I’m an applied mathematician working on PDE/ODE models arising in mathematical biology. My recent work studies critical domain size phenomena and multistability in go-or-grow glioma models, and travelling wave solutions for cooperative and non-cooperative systems. I’m also interested in the bifurcation structure of 2D travelling waves (plane waves, fingering) in nonlocal cell adhesion models.
